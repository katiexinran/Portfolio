Capstone

Matching hiring needs to qualified, diverse candidates. Happy to be Included aims to bridge the gap between diverse hiring needs and finding the most qualified candidates. In today's workplace, diversity is a priority, and recruiters often face the challenge of balancing quality with inclusivity. This project identifies areas lacking diversity and provides practical solutions to source diverse, qualified candidates.
