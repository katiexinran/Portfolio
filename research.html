IG Shopping Usability Study

Light Rail Safety Study

Conducted field studies, interviews, and a survey to investigate how to improve the safety of nighttime light rail commuters.

Redesigning the HCD Process

Proposed revisions to the Empathize, Define, and Test phases of Stanford's Design Thinking methodology to ensure a more inclusive and accurate understanding of user needs.
